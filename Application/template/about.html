About dSummarizer

dSummarizer is a software that is made to minimize effort of movie lovers. As movie lovers have to go through a lot of reviews to find perfect movie. This is time consuming as well as tiring. dSummarizer goes through all the reviews and presents the users with a synopsis. This simple task will enable users to get the crux of the reviews on one click.

Objectives

The principle goal of dSummarizer is to spare the time of movie lovers. Movie lovers will not need to read all reviews of movie. dSummarizer will give synopsis of all reviews of each movie.

Developer's Point

dSummarizer provides developer an healthy environment to get the data easily and also get the synopsis by just use our API.
